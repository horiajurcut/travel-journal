Meet Travel Journal

If you fail to plan, you are planning to fail!

Travel Journal helps you plan your entire trip in under 30 seconds. All you need to do is fill out a simple form, and we take care of the rest. Using the computational power of Google App Engine we collect information, tailored to your preferences, from 11 reliable sources (Google Places, Google Translate, Youtube, Google Books, Flickr, Eventbrite, Expedia, Forecast.io, Wikipedia, Google Maps and Google Street View).

Because it's all about the journey...

Our main focus is not just delivering the most accurate information. We want each and every one of our users to be able to tell their story, in the most amazing way possible! Your future trips are transformed into beautiful, interactive journals that you can share with your friends on Google+, Facebook and Twitter.

Unexplored paths lead to undiscovered treasures

Depending on the information available, you will be able to browse through a variety of places, hotels (with relevant booking information), events, maps, reviews, weather forecast, videos and books. We even added a Translate button, so you don't feel lost when reading reviews in other languages.

Time is an illusion

Travel Journal searches for available hotel rooms, events and weather forecasts around a given time frame. You can go as far as one year into the future. It doesn't matter if you're a planner or if you're spontaneous, now you have all the information you need without any effort. Remember, hope for the best and plan for the worst!

Navigate through the city

Our custom-made Street View navigator lets you explore, step-by-step, the most popular places in a city of your choice. Using the power of Google Maps Direction Service, we create an optimal travel itinerary. If you're in a hurry, this is the best way to visit all the recommended places. Enough talk!
